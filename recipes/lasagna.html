Lasagna

Cheese Meat and Tomato Sauce

Lasagna is an italian dish. Its made by layering tons of delicious tomato Sauce, meat sauce and cheese. It is a savoury dish that aims to please!


Ingredients
  • Cheese (any kind)
  • Tomatoes
  • Pork or Meat
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil


Steps
  1. Fry the tomato sauce
  2. Fry the meat
  3. Mix sauce and meat together
  4. Layer with pasta/meat/cheese
  5. Bake it and done!
