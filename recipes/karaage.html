Karaage

Japanese inspired Fried Chicken

It's light, crispy, marinated with lots of garlic and ginger, covered by potato starch. Amazing snack for movie days


Ingredients
  • Chicken thighs for best effect
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Ginger
  • Potato Starch


Steps
  1. Marinade the Chicken in garlic ginger etc
  2. 30 mins chill in fridge
  3. Coat chicken with starch
  4. Fry Chicken (Hot Oil)
  5. Drain the Oil, Season as Necessary
