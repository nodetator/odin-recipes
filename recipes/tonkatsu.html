Japanese Noodle with Pork Broth

Tonkatsu Ramen is a rich noodle with thick pork broth which really brings out the umami taste inherent in the pork bones used to make the soup


Ingredients
  • Pork Bones
  • Seaweed
  • Tofu
  • Bamboo Shoots
  • Ramen Noodles


Steps
  1. Cook Pork Bone Broth
  2. Make Prawn Sauce
  3. Mix sauce and Broth
  4. Hot Water to Boil your noodles
  5. Serve while hot
